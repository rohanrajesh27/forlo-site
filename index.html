The problem

Your analytics tool works against you
every time someone clicks your ad.

You pay for the click. Then the tool you use to measure it kills the conversion. Here is exactly how.

01
Your page loads too slowly

Standard analytics tools add a heavy JavaScript file to every page load. On mobile, visitors bounce before your page finishes. You pay for clicks that never see your offer.

02
A popup blocks the sale

Cookie-based analytics require a consent banner by law. Someone clicks your ad. A popup blocks them. The moment is gone. That conversion is dead before it starts.

03
You do not own your data

Your analytics sit on someone else's servers. They have changed the product before with no warning. Your traffic history is not yours unless it lives on your server.
