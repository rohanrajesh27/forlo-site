Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025  ·  Effective: June 1, 2025

Forlo ("we", "us", "our") operates forlo.me. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. Read it. It is short and written plainly on purpose.

What we collect from you

When you purchase a Forlo plan, we collect:

  • Your name and email address, provided at checkout
  • Payment information, processed by Stripe. We never see or store your card details
  • Any messages you send us via email

We do not run advertising. We do not sell your data. We do not share your information with third parties except where required to deliver the service (Stripe for payments, your chosen email provider for support correspondence).

What your analytics instance collects

Your Forlo analytics instance runs on your own server. We have no access to your visitor data after setup is complete. Your instance collects:

  • Page views, referrer URLs, and UTM parameters
  • Browser type, device type, and approximate country
  • Session duration and bounce rate

Your instance collects no cookies and no personal identifiers. No IP addresses are stored. No cross-site tracking occurs. Your visitor data belongs to you and lives on your server.

Cookies on forlo.me

Our marketing site does not use tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics. If we use any session cookies for functional purposes (for example, remembering a form state), they are strictly necessary and do not track you across sites.

How we use your information

We use your name and email to:

  • Deliver your setup and send your login credentials
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send transactional emails related to your purchase

We do not send marketing emails without your consent.

Data retention

We retain your purchase records for as long as required by applicable law (typically seven years for financial records). Support correspondence is retained for up to two years. You can request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@forlo.me.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have rights including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@forlo.me. We respond within 30 days.

GDPR and international transfers

If you are located in the European Economic Area, your rights are protected under GDPR. We process data on the basis of contractual necessity (to deliver the service you purchased). We do not transfer your personal data outside your jurisdiction without appropriate safeguards.

Children

Forlo is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have done so inadvertently, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

We update this policy when our practices change. We post the new version here with a revised date. Continued use of Forlo after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@forlo.me. We will respond within 2 business days.
